About Us

Set amidst 42 acres of breathtaking tropical beauty in Maldive's island of Landaa Giraavaru, Kalawu Resort is redefining all-inclusive. Catering to some of the world’s most exclusive clientele from actors Edward Norton and Russell Crowe to fashion icon Donna Karan and NBA Coach Pat Riley, our world-class staff is devoted to providing you an exquisitely luxurious experience unparalleled by any other all-inclusive resort in the Maldives.
